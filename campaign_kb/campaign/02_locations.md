## Location: Draugr’s Hearth System
**Type:** Star system  
**Description (3–5 sentences):** A resource-rich but spiritually cursed system wrapped in the Blazing Choir, where psychic harmonics distort navigation and machine spirits. The system balances volcanic worlds, glacial hives, and an asteroid belt dense with ork wreckage and rogue AI. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
**Tactical features:** Unstable warp weather, navigational hazards, psychic flare events. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
**Threats:** Necron outposts, warp ghosts, pirate salvage teams. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
**Secrets:** The Choir’s signal may amplify or mask the cryo-plague’s psychic vector. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
**Why PCs care:** Control of promethium routes and a system-wide contagion threat. [Source: campaign_seed_doc][Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]
