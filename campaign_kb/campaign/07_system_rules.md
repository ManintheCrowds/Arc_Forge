# House System

## Session Loop
1) Recap from the Harbinger of Woe briefing. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
2) Scene framing focused on kinetic movement and hazards. [Source: campaign_seed_doc]  
3) Consequences tied to the psyker outcome and faction shifts. [Source: campaign_seed_doc]  
4) Rewards tied to promethium access or salvage rights. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
5) Cliffhanger based on Choir flares or cult escalation. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  

## Advancement
- XP rules: Award for decisive action during high-risk mobile encounters. [Source: campaign_seed_doc]  
- Rewards: Use convoy control, promethium, and salvage as primary rewards. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  

## Faction System
- How influence changes: Each mission shifts one faction toward ally/hostile. [Source: campaign_seed_doc][Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
- Thresholds: 3-step track (neutral → leveraged → committed). [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
- Triggers: Psyker fate, convoy losses, public rituals. [Source: campaign_seed_doc]  

## Downtime
- Allowed actions: Crew diplomacy, ship repairs, intel gathering, ritual countermeasures. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]  
- Risks: Ork rivalries, sabotage, machine-spirit instability. [Source: campaign_seed_doc.md]
