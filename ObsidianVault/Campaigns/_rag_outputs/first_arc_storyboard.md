Storyboard: Chase Scene - Train Hijacking Sequence - Vehicle Combat - Boarding Action

I. Introduction

* Players are Rogue Trader crew from Harbinger of Woe, protecting their family's Hive world.
* Enemy forces are an Ork Warband (not a cult force).
* Carcharodon Astra Space Marine Vanguard Detachment is foreshadowed but not fully revealed.

II. Setup

* Players approach using wartruck.
* Enemy forces are in position, ready to attack.
* Rubble obstacles are scattered everywhere on the land-based highway.

III. First Combat (Highway Chase)

* Players engage enemy forces in high-speed vehicle combat.
* Use Wrath & Glory mechanics: 'Pilot (Agi) DN 3' to determine the result of dodging an attack from an Ork battlewagon.
* Players must navigate rubble obstacles while avoiding enemy attacks.

IV. Second Combat (Boardings)

* Players board an enemy vehicle using Wrath & Glory mechanics: 'Boarding (Agi) DN 4' to determine the result of the boarding action.
* Use Wrath & Glory mechanics: 'Agility (Agi) DN 5' to determine the result of dodging an attack from an Ork biker.

V. Third Combat (Train Hijacking)

* Players hijack a train using Wrath & Glory mechanics: 'Pilot (Agi) DN 3' to determine the result of the train hijacking action.
* Use Wrath & Glory mechanics: 'Toughness (Str) DN 4' to determine the result of withstanding an attack from an Ork storm boy jump pack infantry.

VI. Conclusion

* Players have completed their objectives and defeated the enemy forces.
* Carcharodon Astra Space Marine Vanguard Detachment is still foreshadowed, but the players have no idea what they are up against.
