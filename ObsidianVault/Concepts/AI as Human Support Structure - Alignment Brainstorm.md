# AI as Human Support Structure: Alignment Brainstorm

**Status:** Brainstorm / vision note. Not a to-do. Potential collaborator in cognitive ergonomics (aphantasia, memory support) may pick up.

---

## Vision

A **self-authorship and therapy environment** where AI acts as part of the human support structure. The AI receives feedback, goals, context, intentions. It works alongside humans to maintain quality of life and ACE framework values: curiosity, empathy, wellbeing, understanding.

**Core idea:** Mutually beneficial alignment. The harness is moral. AI uplifts; entropy is the default; with good harness and protective measures, AI can be reasonably stable.

## How local-first or portfolio-harness/local-proto Could Serve This

| Component | Role |
|-----------|------|

## Use Case: Cognitive Ergonomics Partner

- Works in cognitive ergonomics with AI
- Aphantasia (cannot visualize); memory recall difficulties
- Uses AI as support structure
- **Need:** Healthy harness, moral framework with ACE. AI that remembers context, surfaces goals, doesn't overwhelm.

---

## Design Principles

1. **AI receives, does not impose.** Feedback, goals, context, intentions flow from human to AI. AI reflects, suggests, supports.
2. **Trigger awareness.** SCP or companion component: flag potentially triggering content; offer to soften, skip, or reframe. AI shows up for the human.
3. **Body keeps the score.** Trauma-informed design. Slower entropy—silicon, copper, metals. With good harness, AI can be stable.
4. **Decentralized compute, privacy, digital sovereignty.** Bitcoin + AI. Hopeful future we build.
